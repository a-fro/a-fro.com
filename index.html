Meeting this Moment

When we go on retreat, what are we retreating from? And in the afterglow of a retreat, how do we respond to the inevitable ups and downs of the challenges we face to re-integrate? Though these are difficult questions to answer, the more beautiful world our hearts know is possible is waiting for us. This essay explores how we can bring the essence of retreat back into our daily lives.
